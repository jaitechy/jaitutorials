    Start Growing With Us

    Jai Tutorials is an Educational Institute conducted coaching classes for 1st To 12th (CBSE and ICSE Board.)

    Our mission is to develop youth with active and creative minds ,a sense of understanding and compassion for others the courage to act on their beliefs.

    We stress the total development of each child: spiritual, moral, intellectual, social & emotional.

    Procedure of Coaching

    Jai Tutorials is promoted with a view to impact quality education with the help of dedicated and expert faculty members.

    • Daily Classes are held (from Monday to Saturday.)
    • All the subjects are taught.
    • Each topic of each subject well described with easy technique so that even the weakest student can understand.
    • Test is conducted on every Saturday.
